About us

As a class, we have really come together. We are very forward thinking adults, and like to include everyone within our ranks.
When it comes to computers, we are accepting of ALL choices..... as long as that choice is a MAC.
In order to explain WHY we feel this way, we will need to use a pros and cons list for both the superior MAC and the vastly inferior PC.

    Pros for PC
  1. The track pad has an actual right click
  2. Good for gaming
  3. ... is there anything else?
    Cons for PC
  1. Runs Windows
  2. It isn't a mac
  3. Not a superior mac
    Pros for MAC
  1. It isn't a PC
  2. EVERY CODER IS THE WORLD USES A MAC
  3. Easy to use (so we've heard)
    Cons for MAC
  1. Not the best for gaming
  2. Are there any other cons...?

In closing, please don't be afraid of choosing what ever computer suits you more. It's all personal preference. We just need you to understand that if you don't choose a MAC you are dead to us!
